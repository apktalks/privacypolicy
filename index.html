Privacy Policy

At Apktalks, we are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information in relation to your use of our apps offered under the Apktalks platform.

By using any app provided by Apktalks, you acknowledge and agree that all personal data or information collected while using the app is stored solely on your device. Apktalks does not collect, store, or have access to this information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data directly from users. Any information provided or generated while using the app is stored locally on your device. We do not store or transmit any personal information to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We work with third-party services to enhance the performance, analytics, and functionality of our apps. These third-party services may collect certain types of data directly from the app for the purposes of improving performance, user experience, and analytics. This data is transmitted directly to their servers and is not collected by Apktalks.

These third-party services may collect data such as:

  • Device type and operating system;
  • App usage information and crash logs;
  • Performance analytics to optimize the app experience.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services for a better understanding of how your data is handled. The services may include, but are not limited to, analytics providers, crash reporting tools, and cloud storage providers.

Usage Data

When you interact with our apps, certain information about your device and app usage may be collected automatically by third-party services we use for performance analysis. This data may include details such as your device type, operating system, IP address, time of usage, and crash reports. However, all such data is sent directly to third-party servers and is not stored by Apktalks.

Protecting Your Information

Your privacy is our priority, and although we do not collect or store your personal data, the third-party services we use may have access to some of your information as described above. These services implement their own security measures to protect your data, but we cannot guarantee the security of data transmitted over the internet or stored electronically by these third parties.

Links to External Websites

Our apps may include links to external websites or services. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices or content on these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with through links in our apps.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal data to any third-party service used by our app, we will take immediate steps to delete such information from the relevant third-party databases. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has shared personal data with a third-party service through our apps, please contact us at apktalkshelp@gmail.com so we can assist in the removal of such data.

Policy Changes

Our Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our apps or legal requirements. We encourage you to check this page from time to time for any updates. All changes to this policy will be posted here, and they take effect as soon as they are published.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out to us at apktalkshelp@gmail.com. We’re here to help.
